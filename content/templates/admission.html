Admission Requirements (2018/2019 Year)

Admission fee plus School fess equals Ghc 800.00 (Mode of payment negotiable)

Things to be supplied to child after admission:

  • Set of text books
  • 2 sets of uniform
  • 1 Lacoste
  • 1 set of crayon
  • 1 file
  • Purified water for the term

Also, Parents of a newly admitted child would have to provide the following toiletries:

  • 2 baby wipes
  • 2 mini sunlight soaps
  • Medium sized washing powder
  • A Camel Detergent
