Overview of Amazing Grace Montessori School

A Christian School founded on 14th September, 2015, with the core value of educating children in the fear of the Lord.

Our Mission

To provide the child with the opportunity to acquire an education founded on the educational philosophy of Dr. Maria Montessori blended with the Ghana Education Service curricular and the Good News of our Lord Jesus Christ.
